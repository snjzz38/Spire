University Hub Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 9, 2025

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to help us understand how our site is being used and how we can improve it. This includes information like page views, country of origin, and browser type. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or IP address.

2. How We Use Information

The anonymous data collected is used solely for internal analysis to improve the website's content and user experience. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer this information to outside parties.

3. Data Storage (Interactive Timeline)

For our Interactive Timeline feature, task data is stored locally in your browser's `localStorage`. This data is not transmitted to our servers and is accessible only by you on your own device. Clearing your browser's cache or storage will permanently delete this data.

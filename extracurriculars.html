Extracurriculars

Beyond your grades and test scores, your extracurricular activities paint a picture of who you are, what you're passionate about, and how you'll contribute to a university community.

Extracurriculars Ranked by Prestige (Tiers)

Admissions officers often think about extracurriculars in terms of tiers of achievement and impact. While there's no official system, this is a helpful way to understand what makes an activity stand out.

  • Tier 1: Exceptional and Rare Achievements These are activities that demonstrate exceptional talent and are rare for a high school student. They often involve national or international recognition. Examples include being a highly recruited athlete, winning a prestigious national award (like the Regeneron Science Talent Search), or founding a successful non-profit that gains significant media attention.[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEoriF-YLGt3emhkyfbR6XpggArTLhFV7RnUJMVEkSoc-IUJviuAeffOmZQnCQrBi8SK45EPG0OwkuNYo7KtNouQ0ECQT20d-MX6EyOeb8xEiUReczoJsO4VbvxkW9_Pc7xKGfGhVNMFy_pefhuk5-Bwg3x8cpDwX90CxezYvn-8ZAtIEU%3D)][[2](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjbiotSGWwGFajYUV2ORuAEv7kv2mLTAcobjulzplbrIw3_MSN3vOa8bFDnIcMT2eMTrkFQXvrkqlNjm5fF_faGVWxkymfBxFEVAmW56-qFYD1bPqikcmxgDdk4H1dw_3vWtt_ITRNLklubJcL5VR_J9bWX2KR5ooHEUtUzUkW0Bc0sHt7dJ09YpPXxp27Dw%3D%3D)][[3](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGjE0V9Vzph6eDmPn_8Cvusezl_yJSIfguhDlLykdjRGrIVG4zsVidozirbLmeCFMP1hD6QRSChEZCFDB0mtczN_PRZrAVMwvYi09ffp3DUsCMPxOnir84gkHtcewD7Jtx7pH2Q9Qe_LxYK4Nmy5a0Nc1QMnZFKspZ9)]
  • Tier 2: High-Level Achievement and Leadership These activities are more common than Tier 1 but still show significant accomplishment. This could include holding a major leadership position in a well-respected club (like Model UN president), achieving all-state recognition in sports or music, or winning a regional competition.[[2](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjbiotSGWwGFajYUV2ORuAEv7kv2mLTAcobjulzplbrIw3_MSN3vOa8bFDnIcMT2eMTrkFQXvrkqlNjm5fF_faGVWxkymfBxFEVAmW56-qFYD1bPqikcmxgDdk4H1dw_3vWtt_ITRNLklubJcL5VR_J9bWX2KR5ooHEUtUzUkW0Bc0sHt7dJ09YpPXxp27Dw%3D%3D)][[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQHydFByMsaRpJovbbwbceegLoNG3NpCPHrObA-ew2fUJGhv5o7lxoZuPaBvR-GcU4cg-ty25PVgXnf0T1bnUy5erA7IdCpjaTnRJIt7TElJA8CgA-7Hp3guZFqdKrIGXdKXZNkYd0rVKTXEJTSOkiK1nfvXh3WAaJtAZgVp)]
  • Tier 3: Active Participation and Minor Leadership This tier includes meaningful participation in clubs and activities. Examples are holding a minor leadership role (like treasurer), being a dedicated member of a sports team, or consistent volunteering for a cause you care about.[[2](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjbiotSGWwGFajYUV2ORuAEv7kv2mLTAcobjulzplbrIw3_MSN3vOa8bFDnIcMT2eMTrkFQXvrkqlNjm5fF_faGVWxkymfBxFEVAmW56-qFYD1bPqikcmxgDdk4H1dw_3vWtt_ITRNLklubJcL5VR_J9bWX2KR5ooHEUtUzUkW0Bc0sHt7dJ09YpPXxp27Dw%3D%3D)][[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQHydFByMsaRpJovbbwbceegLoNG3NpCPHrObA-ew2fUJGhv5o7lxoZuPaBvR-GcU4cg-ty25PVgXnf0T1bnUy5erA7IdCpjaTnRJIt7TElJA8CgA-7Hp3guZFqdKrIGXdKXZNkYd0rVKTXEJTSOkiK1nfvXh3WAaJtAZgVp)]
  • Tier 4: General Membership and Participation These are the most common activities and include general membership in clubs without a specific leadership role or sporadic volunteer work. While not as impactful as higher-tiered activities, they still show a willingness to be involved.[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEoriF-YLGt3emhkyfbR6XpggArTLhFV7RnUJMVEkSoc-IUJviuAeffOmZQnCQrBi8SK45EPG0OwkuNYo7KtNouQ0ECQT20d-MX6EyOeb8xEiUReczoJsO4VbvxkW9_Pc7xKGfGhVNMFy_pefhuk5-Bwg3x8cpDwX90CxezYvn-8ZAtIEU%3D)][[3](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGjE0V9Vzph6eDmPn_8Cvusezl_yJSIfguhDlLykdjRGrIVG4zsVidozirbLmeCFMP1hD6QRSChEZCFDB0mtczN_PRZrAVMwvYi09ffp3DUsCMPxOnir84gkHtcewD7Jtx7pH2Q9Qe_LxYK4Nmy5a0Nc1QMnZFKspZ9)]

Finding Your Profile: Spike, Well-Rounded, or Interdisciplinary?

There are different strategies for building your extracurricular profile. Understanding these can help you decide where to focus your energy.

üéØ The "Spike" Profile

A "spike" refers to developing a deep and impressive expertise in one particular area.[[5](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQF2yra1MkWnjAeYzyU5D0RjZ1Vx0MCUD-CCXwyyOwUdaDi2QizETsxsszw0yxd-eWY4UQQUzuMAiVMzZeFUiQEKytML6PkoL-kLxoaquekync_qwFG5pl4mpe_FH9FRWUSN4KnoSKJDbFQCOjzuriIvFzM-EXHKLkf_zbqFyseWXeWmiVAK)][[6](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQHKXDlUI_YPT8BxwB_6i0VowFOZBxYpzsunE5T1Sd0kHd6d4BWPqVYFsOAK_gyqZnbrFUDIG_hGdHsyDSEbvm5VJ1aKeq0bozW72pJhBqgIat81p0KMQcvgXjr3xWtNRG--9GaFXpyhktUs9Y_Xp27xXQvMibwY5wZXx3RTfQ6b5R7_1c6iDbpsw6CK7QfpBJjEcGjghAmLXg%3D%3D)] Instead of participating in many different activities, you focus your time and energy on one or two passions. This could be anything from competitive coding to scientific research or playing a musical instrument at a high level. Highly selective universities often look for students with spikes to create a diverse class of specialists.[[5](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQF2yra1MkWnjAeYzyU5D0RjZ1Vx0MCUD-CCXwyyOwUdaDi2QizETsxsszw0yxd-eWY4UQQUzuMAiVMzZeFUiQEKytML6PkoL-kLxoaquekync_qwFG5pl4mpe_FH9FRWUSN4KnoSKJDbFQCOjzuriIvFzM-EXHKLkf_zbqFyseWXeWmiVAK)][[7](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEwqkapY4l_pR2oBwZSCE8B_JdvhYKC7fQ5I3oGWWGyPndizUZeAFuiWyKkLN3J4PEvoJ8T3SyhDA6WGS2RteI48TCr3-XctJ3NZkDkNW9g4uKhqsGmXPCitkpq5ihWBj-yeDJi2A%3D%3D)][[8](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQHikHGu3VO_c9rWEhMe4ETEw_2mziUFC1Kih6RiSPdFJXYtPwBHvJXnqlJcjnpZQRSzeIWuvNANrppqyp9PDI-tX2HnhJ3A_4KguR6cbMvptT8gIdIqszqVTcYPs20gUVXaCfO2LbIOxFfSkug4vIl0QQXBVo8ocQnMokepk9KfQflWkThiyhVwmynGnPMb4BsqVsIoct3jFZRUZoaSck8R_FONVWgJf6TmGmQY1m6t73LJRFCaoVTP)] For example, a student with a spike in computer science might have won hackathons, developed an app, and interned at a tech company.[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEoriF-YLGt3emhkyfbR6XpggArTLhFV7RnUJMVEkSoc-IUJviuAeffOmZQnCQrBi8SK45EPG0OwkuNYo7KtNouQ0ECQT20d-MX6EyOeb8xEiUReczoJsO4VbvxkW9_Pc7xKGfGhVNMFy_pefhuk5-Bwg3x8cpDwX90CxezYvn-8ZAtIEU%3D)][[2](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjbiotSGWwGFajYUV2ORuAEv7kv2mLTAcobjulzplbrIw3_MSN3vOa8bFDnIcMT2eMTrkFQXvrkqlNjm5fF_faGVWxkymfBxFEVAmW56-qFYD1bPqikcmxgDdk4H1dw_3vWtt_ITRNLklubJcL5VR_J9bWX2KR5ooHEUtUzUkW0Bc0sHt7dJ09YpPXxp27Dw%3D%3D)]

üåç The Well-Rounded Profile

The well-rounded applicant participates in a variety of activities, demonstrating a broad range of interests and abilities. This might include playing a sport, being in the school play, volunteering, and being a member of several clubs. While the idea of being "well-rounded" is sometimes seen as less effective for top-tier schools that prefer spikes, it can still be a successful strategy, especially if you show leadership and commitment across your activities.[[9](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGsu4oV3CMWT-qqPGwD7D3RnhQMFX19qMZxjT6JkJGAPexiEjc6zslbRb8lrAC-wnqatWdNQFboT9Afn1HnOnt72h36slsl2nyuKB6qaQIkhlnaaLIdw2XWXxgBVpza1Rgd7leB40dlk0GrmLVwsjRUb1UMCdnoyWbrzEIowfN_P5m5JJto7sI_peJ8)][[10](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFUidhI4Aqvwt690qMg5uBETbo4XTx5hOTaeZmdiretyP4z8IG3BDK3l96K1w1p4yEABp3HUn1WlGJQ7s_DE-NerGHynA2lohUi5m8XFRgsOWWyaFNuzFjoJ2fEdfxW0FCG4UBtcckB4Jf0i7gYv9BBCuybstsAgiiNJpt-W2nZ)] The key is to avoid looking like a "jack of all trades, but a master of none."[[7](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEwqkapY4l_pR2oBwZSCE8B_JdvhYKC7fQ5I3oGWWGyPndizUZeAFuiWyKkLN3J4PEvoJ8T3SyhDA6WGS2RteI48TCr3-XctJ3NZkDkNW9g4uKhqsGmXPCitkpq5ihWBj-yeDJi2A%3D%3D)][[11](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFJUYpXgC8bs8I8XAxmSqxywfV7rBgVfYrTcJzunqWreEFp95e2gMEbkzbVxjL8xfVDVimx648-5Oc98bOkNSwGZ6XzpVfoTGHIcwcqsMHDFBx_qiGfrh7G46YzJMuRL2g7R74lJexy1r8FBD9H8sPMP4FBLQZpPMGvsku8whP0g4gr6phQ7PI7o1nMM3aRU8Xjzbb3yaCrlofYB7tYr7PmyHAu7EXVvplm_nZGaxAXKAG82lxPdCHIfu8ySlpjSe5zFDqYL-ylWKstGHy4zbZfXk1lOQqA)]

ü§ù The Interdisciplinary Profile

An interdisciplinary approach involves combining interests from different fields to create a unique niche.[[12](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEiWEWSPhuEqCxjgbmV47qJAH2pt66P47upjbj-DxjmAzjdeXFFM4N-l_cyvc_BK18JI0DcIEq97uIzVQ4gPkLDaU4fCk0wb_8lsAtrNHRJ6IukNsiX1CR4P3BK2JRs-Mz5EtZE5-0eKFRzLmV4sbMRxcbruxhVLl-jZc_1RV0nQ43HjXApXSkwnPlk-mFsxpkoi_OCBciNdDF--IRPLI8OVDIIYGqsT9AZChlk_dCICYMFNOMfb9jtIMIQ8LXLSlSPve6mDY3fw4tbZItrlxCJbozA7LLr)][[13](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQG8Tugj0HZC9lclkwTsw4zPrzYJL5Ywsps3SY2GzFX_mR_kABKce0gx2hb-o3nCFMaildCsu9iV4-XeWKCPE_bpZjFdjeZQHk36EODU5VZrKIsKlm6hdnQTD3pPz3TKfXZI46TZGD_OpDhSgXo_Ln3qx0gscAH-ePM1qjM9eqhPR6JrRFaSaoBEeSh2XG5XFDaSorpcaLARl9SBbwCSaXFLHS_M_72oF0CQDZBf)] For example, a student passionate about both computer science and art could create a project on digital art or use coding to analyze artistic trends. This shows creativity, innovation, and the ability to think across traditional subject boundaries.[[3](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGjE0V9Vzph6eDmPn_8Cvusezl_yJSIfguhDlLykdjRGrIVG4zsVidozirbLmeCFMP1hD6QRSChEZCFDB0mtczN_PRZrAVMwvYi09ffp3DUsCMPxOnir84gkHtcewD7Jtx7pH2Q9Qe_LxYK4Nmy5a0Nc1QMnZFKspZ9)][[12](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEiWEWSPhuEqCxjgbmV47qJAH2pt66P47upjbj-DxjmAzjdeXFFM4N-l_cyvc_BK18JI0DcIEq97uIzVQ4gPkLDaU4fCk0wb_8lsAtrNHRJ6IukNsiX1CR4P3BK2JRs-Mz5EtZE5-0eKFRzLmV4sbMRxcbruxhVLl-jZc_1RV0nQ43HjXApXSkwnPlk-mFsxpkoi_OCBciNdDF--IRPLI8OVDIIYGqsT9AZChlk_dCICYMFNOMfb9jtIMIQ8LXLSlSPve6mDY3fw4tbZItrlxCJbozA7LLr)] This can be a powerful way to stand out and demonstrate a unique perspective.[[12](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEiWEWSPhuEqCxjgbmV47qJAH2pt66P47upjbj-DxjmAzjdeXFFM4N-l_cyvc_BK18JI0DcIEq97uIzVQ4gPkLDaU4fCk0wb_8lsAtrNHRJ6IukNsiX1CR4P3BK2JRs-Mz5EtZE5-0eKFRzLmV4sbMRxcbruxhVLl-jZc_1RV0nQ43HjXApXSkwnPlk-mFsxpkoi_OCBciNdDF--IRPLI8OVDIIYGqsT9AZChlk_dCICYMFNOMfb9jtIMIQ8LXLSlSPve6mDY3fw4tbZItrlxCJbozA7LLr)][[13](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQG8Tugj0HZC9lclkwTsw4zPrzYJL5Ywsps3SY2GzFX_mR_kABKce0gx2hb-o3nCFMaildCsu9iV4-XeWKCPE_bpZjFdjeZQHk36EODU5VZrKIsKlm6hdnQTD3pPz3TKfXZI46TZGD_OpDhSgXo_Ln3qx0gscAH-ePM1qjM9eqhPR6JrRFaSaoBEeSh2XG5XFDaSorpcaLARl9SBbwCSaXFLHS_M_72oF0CQDZBf)]

What's the Right Balance?

There is no single "correct" ratio of activities. The best approach is to pursue what genuinely interests you.[[14](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFr32QkiuqXsSYdP4cpbJpwTqPpf4pE72_bYfu4ylURsTAUP2H_PXmM7FBEunROz_2Q74ihvuaR-9oMXkFMpSpIlM6F82KGOrNRE4UMcXjCQNUjWriK1YWgkmmixGFZtpzOdYS6vAUZiGiKwSV53ejM2pTb263eyutqTTz-GdPhEuLgvL9I0egZDUUohMZmnnwp15mOEcYQE7qnlg%3D%3D)][[15](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEQYYEOwMxXVrhSDHuYd9_n-atyegJOwPH-0Dk-27cEvEyslYClfK99QphhkOTzxVpZprO8JqjbvOtwaUDefOK1bmirtNf5WbVMiImC45uEJAjTK9U8Wa5pOR8QUVQoRTVPD5qNJ6Bqr6G0LnX_MJk26Dju4sgZsieHbC353IdDgBmxjKX9FPMTtz6_TNRlaHmnXtIzqrWZ86xW9i70_Eqy6gD3ZjnVi3u4efimfkZNZQHhuvfexA%3D%3D)] However, here are some general guidelines:

  • Depth over Breadth: It's generally better to have deep involvement in a few activities than superficial involvement in many.[[16](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEvINB1b1rpyTpHuoEfc8k_K7rjbsI3eo8kfR0S6KFaAO1Ike5bnqrK51P1ZdGKBxOnAfhRLrjVHBVsH685TKNNeFYf6IS1KBbay6EHHmM7i-7qvv6t0YxronZ4cTcXoJVVv5xyCDSh3yOBhm3qJE45nigoO6RB-hZ4T_OtZdfB54IKwJ9_h_ne8aXtHGVRvXhiYrNR12ZSN47IcSRkSr8tApnfjrkN0DqwokI%3D)][[17](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGCU4B6IFQUM-BvcEBilMZkcTlG8RGvnKctTGzOQPtmJl-xi8ASEvw0yY_lZMfs51fKM1Ynx0OwMXgkVi4CZBICyT2iLNM6xdmXe5AlpCUqJ8p6bXV57wifU2j5dtf67kcGg8GwZnZpXlL_TyM39srMBb-lUWOZwE1o1pYBIPScLJ2G2D19ppT8LjzUUP5dI5ZlsN9RNBe5uIg%3D)]
  • Show, Don't Just Tell: If you're passionate about something, your activities should reflect that. A student interested in medicine might volunteer at a hospital, conduct related research, and lead a health-focused club.
  • Leadership Matters: Leadership doesn't always mean being president. It can be taking the initiative on a project, mentoring younger students, or organizing an event.[[18](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGhkljUVZosQuHTq6tXAr7B4hiKF6pgPbP7bnpjZApG_jqL8xhL8Hm2xySJr4twHLAADK4G1VKQP4ShPWVh2571QXi-9Sg4c2TR5jD-DyIIBjM-S2WUe2FijuUzINnrdH9uB9eIaDNkAZKBk4Jq8XrEdWEUoXuED7pRQrHuf4zZfKo1OGPx2DyrSOYY6s000Z24M_Xyf-Xq_TnzsnyHMrnpgBaH5oc1ueWKqyff)]

Ultimately, the most compelling extracurricular profile is one that is authentic to you. Your activities should tell a story about your passions, your commitment, and the unique qualities you'll bring to a college campus.
